PORTAL TIMESTALL

Portal Timestall is an early in development free modification for Portal 2 that takes place
in the future of Aperture Science after a time travel incident spirals the facility into chaos.

Show Me More!

A Brand New Testing Element

Aperture Presents: The Time Button! A brand new invention that temporarily slows down time of objects. Use the Time button to reach places you couldn't before, pick up speedy objects and perform advanced movement, the possibilities are endless.

A Brand New Story

This isn't the Aperture you're used to. After the protagonist gets involved in time travel testing gone wrong
they find themselves in the future and with every new timeline comes a new business venture for Aperture,
it just so happens this one is in Military weaponry. After their huge success selling weapons to the government
Aperture's old testing track was left neglected and forgotten. Too bad you need to go through the tests to
make your way to the Time Core in order to reset the timeline and get home. It's not all bad though! Your handy dandy
new Portal Gun is equipped with an advanced Artificial Intelligence which means you can have peace of mind knowing
that, if it were possible, he would help you through the tests.
